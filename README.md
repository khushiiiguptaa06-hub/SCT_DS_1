# SCT_DS_1 - Student Mental Health Analysis

##  Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze and visualize student mental health trends using data visualization techniques. The analysis focuses on factors such as gender, age, academic background, depression, anxiety, panic attacks, and treatment-seeking behavior.

---

##  Dataset

The dataset contains survey responses from students regarding their demographic information and mental health status.

### Features Included

* Gender
* Age
* Course
* Year of Study
* CGPA
* Marital Status
* Depression
* Anxiety
* Panic Attacks
* Specialist Treatment

---

##  Tools & Libraries Used

* Python
* Pandas
* NumPy
* Matplotlib
* Seaborn
* Google Colab

---

##  Data Analysis Process

### 1. Data Understanding

* Examined dataset structure and dimensions
* Reviewed column information
* Generated statistical summaries

### 2. Data Cleaning

* Checked for missing values
* Checked for duplicate records
* Verified data consistency

### 3. Data Visualization

The following visualizations were created:

* Gender Distribution
* Age Distribution
* Depression Distribution
* Anxiety Distribution
* Depression by Gender
* Anxiety by Gender
* Depression by Year of Study
* Top 10 Courses by Student Count
* Relationship Between Depression and Panic Attacks (Heatmap)

---

##  Key Insights

* Most respondents belonged to the typical university-age group.
* Anxiety and depression were reported by a significant number of students.
* Mental health patterns varied across genders and academic years.
* Students experiencing depression showed a higher tendency to report panic attacks.
* The majority of students did not report severe mental health concerns, but a notable portion required attention and support.

---

##  Conclusion

This analysis provides valuable insights into student mental health and highlights the importance of awareness, support systems, and early intervention. Data visualization helped uncover meaningful trends related to depression, anxiety, and panic attacks among students.
